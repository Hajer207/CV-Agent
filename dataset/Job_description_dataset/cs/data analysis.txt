Job Purpose :

To support business operations through data analysis, reporting, and insights generation, enabling informed decision-making and continuous improvement of operational performance.

Accountabilities :

Collect, analyze, and interpret operational data to identify trends and insights.
Develop and maintain dashboards and reports to monitor business performance.
Support decision-making by providing data-driven recommendations.
Track and analyze key operational KPIs and performance metrics.
Ensure data accuracy, consistency, and integrity across reporting outputs.
Identify opportunities for process improvement based on data analysis
Assist in automating reporting and improving data workflows.
Prepare reports and presentations for management review.

Requirements
Bachelor’s degree in Data Analytics, Business, Statistics, or related field.
0 - 1 years of experience in data analysis, business operations, or reporting roles
