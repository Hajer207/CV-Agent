Job Title: IT Support Specialist
Company: Digital Tech Solutions
Location: Dammam, Saudi Arabia
Employment Type: Full-Time
Salary: 9,000 - 13,000 SAR per month

Job Summary:
Digital Tech Solutions is seeking an IT Support Specialist to provide technical support and maintain company systems, devices, and networks. The candidate will troubleshoot hardware and software issues while ensuring efficient IT operations across departments.

Key Responsibilities:
- Provide technical support for hardware, software, and network issues.
- Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and applications.
- Troubleshoot connectivity and system performance problems.
- Support cybersecurity and system update activities.
- Monitor IT systems and maintain technical documentation.
- Assist employees with technical requests and troubleshooting.
- Configure printers, devices, and workstations.
- Collaborate with IT teams to improve system performance.

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or a related field.
- Knowledge of computer systems, operating systems, and networking concepts.
- Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills.
- Basic understanding of cybersecurity concepts.
- Good communication and teamwork skills.
- Ability to work under pressure and resolve technical issues efficiently.

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with Windows and Linux systems.
- Familiarity with networking and system administration.
- Knowledge of ticketing systems and IT support tools.

Benefits:
- Health insurance
- Technical training opportunities
- Annual bonus
- Paid annual leave

HR Screening Notes:
The strongest candidates should demonstrate technical troubleshooting abilities, communication skills, networking knowledge, and basic cybersecurity awareness.
