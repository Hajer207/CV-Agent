Job Title: Accountant
Company: Future Finance Solutions
Location: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia
Employment Type: Full-Time
Salary: 8,000 - 11,000 SAR per month

Job Summary:
Future Finance Solutions is seeking a detail-oriented Accountant to join our growing finance department. The Accountant will be responsible for maintaining accurate financial records, preparing reports, supporting budgeting activities, and ensuring compliance with company financial policies. The ideal candidate should possess strong analytical abilities, organizational skills, and attention to detail.

Key Responsibilities:
- Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports.
- Record financial transactions and maintain accounting documentation.
- Reconcile bank accounts and financial statements.
- Support budgeting and forecasting activities.
- Monitor company expenses and assist in cost analysis.
- Ensure compliance with financial regulations and company policies.
- Collaborate with internal departments regarding financial processes.
- Assist during internal and external audits.

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
- Minimum 1 year of accounting experience.
- Strong knowledge of accounting principles and financial reporting.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and accounting systems.
- Good analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Strong attention to detail and organizational abilities.
- Good communication and teamwork skills.

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with ERP systems.
- Familiarity with Power BI or financial dashboards.
- Knowledge of budgeting and forecasting processes.

Benefits:
- Health insurance
- Annual performance bonus
- Professional development opportunities
- Paid annual leave

HR Screening Notes:
The strongest candidates should demonstrate strong Excel skills, accounting accuracy, analytical thinking, reporting experience, and the ability to work under deadlines.
