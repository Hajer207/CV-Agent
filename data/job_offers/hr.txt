Job Title: HR Specialist
Company: Smart Workforce Solutions
Location: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia
Employment Type: Full-Time
Salary: 7,000 - 10,000 SAR per month

Job Summary:
Smart Workforce Solutions is looking for an HR Specialist to support recruitment, onboarding, employee relations, and HR operations. The successful candidate will work closely with department managers to support hiring needs and maintain a positive work environment.

Key Responsibilities:
- Manage recruitment and interview scheduling processes.
- Conduct candidate screening and HR interviews.
- Coordinate onboarding and employee orientation activities.
- Maintain employee records and HR documentation.
- Assist in implementing HR policies and procedures.
- Support employee engagement and communication initiatives.
- Handle HR administrative tasks and reporting.
- Collaborate with managers regarding staffing requirements.

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.
- Experience in recruitment or HR operations is preferred.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Good organizational and time management abilities.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications.
- Ability to handle confidential information professionally.

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with HR systems or ATS platforms.
- Problem-solving and conflict resolution skills.
- Team collaboration and communication abilities.

Benefits:
- Health insurance
- Career development opportunities
- Flexible working environment
- Paid annual leave

HR Screening Notes:
The strongest candidates should demonstrate professionalism, communication skills, recruitment experience, and the ability to manage HR operations efficiently.
